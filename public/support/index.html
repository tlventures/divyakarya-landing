Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the panchanga data?

Our calculations use the Swiss Ephemeris library, the same engine used by professional astrologers worldwide. Accuracy is within seconds for planetary positions.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Core panchanga calculations (tithi, nakshatra, yoga, karana, sunrise/sunset, hora) work completely offline. Internet is only needed for stotras, horoscopes, and ads.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings (gear icon) > Language. The app supports English, Telugu, Tamil, Gujarati, and Marathi.

How do I change my location?

Tap the location pin on the home screen header to search and select any city worldwide.

What is Tarabalam and Chandrashtama?

Tarabalam checks the compatibility of the current nakshatra with your birth nakshatra. Chandrashtama indicates when the Moon transits the 8th house from your birth Moon sign. Add family members to see personalized results.

How do I share the daily panchanga?

Scroll to the bottom of the Daily Panchang screen and tap "Share Today's Panchanga". A beautiful image card will be generated and shared.

Is my data safe?

All your personal data (name, family details, preferences) is stored only on your device. We do not send any personal information to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I use the AI Pandit?

Navigate to the AI Pandit section from the app menu. You can ask questions about Vedic traditions, panchanga meanings, rituals, and more. Internet connection is required for this feature.
